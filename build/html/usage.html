How to Use

Selecting Your Spot: Log in to the Parktimesaver platform, choose your desired date, time, and location to see available parking spots. Booking: Once you find a suitable spot, you can book it instantly with just a few clicks.

Confirmation and Reminders: After booking, you’ll receive a confirmation. We also send reminders as your reserved time approaches. Modifications: Plans change, and so can your reservations. You can modify or cancel your booking through the app or website.

Paying Online: After using the parking lot, you can make payments online through our secure payment gateway. Receipts and History: You’ll receive digital receipts for your payments, and you can access your payment history anytime for convenience.
